Disaster Dos and Don'ts

Earthquake

Dos

  • Find cover and take shelter.
  • Stay indoors until the shaking stops.
  • Drop, cover, and hold on during shaking.

Don'ts

  • Don't use elevators.
  • Don't run outside during shaking.
  • Don't stand near windows or heavy objects.

Floods

Dos

  • Listen to emergency alerts and evacuate if advised.
  • Move to higher ground.
  • Have an emergency kit ready.

Don'ts

  • Don't walk or drive through floodwaters.
  • Don't ignore evacuation orders.
  • Don't touch electrical equipment in flooded areas.

Hurricane

Dos

  • Stay informed through weather updates.
  • Secure your home and belongings.
  • Have a disaster kit prepared.

Don'ts

  • Don't stay in a mobile home.
  • Don't go outside during the storm.
  • Don't use candles for lighting.
